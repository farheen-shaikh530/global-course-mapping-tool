## Project Overview
The **Course Mapping System** was developed for the **University of the Pacific ‚Äì Office of Global Education** (Summer 2025).  
It streamlines the process of **searching, mapping, and approving international courses** for students preparing to study abroad.  

This system enables students and staff to easily view previously approved courses, reduces redundant paperwork, and ensures transparent decision-making for outbound study abroad participants.  

---

## Problem Statement
Before this project, students and staff struggled with:  
- Limited visibility into **previously approved courses**.  
- Redundant submissions for course approvals.  
- Confusing, inconsistent UI that made it difficult to evaluate international credits.  

---

## Users & Customer Needs
**Primary users:** Study abroad students, academic advisors, and office coordinators.  
**Key needs:**  
- Fast and transparent course approval.  
- A simple interface aligned with university branding.  
- Exportable results for advisors and students.  

---

## My Role
- **Full-Stack Developer & UX Designer**  
- Designed wireframes, user flows, and final UI aligned with **University of the Pacific Brand Guidelines** ([link](https://www.pacific.edu/sites/default/files/users/user245/UPac_BrandGuidelines_Final_compressed.pdf)).  
- Implemented search, filter, and course approval workflows using **React (JavaScript, HTML, CSS)** and Google Sheets API.  
- Applied **UX best practices**: clear hierarchy, responsive design, and accessible interactions.  
- Collaborated with the **Study Abroad Office, Media & Design Department, and student testers** in Agile sprints.  
- Conducted training sessions for staff to update data sources and maintain the system after deployment.  

---

## Design Process
1. **Research & Discovery** ‚Äì interviewed students and advisors about course approval pain points.  
2. **Wireframing & Iteration** ‚Äì built low-fi wireframes in **Figma**, refined through weekly feedback sessions.  
3. **Implementation** ‚Äì created a responsive web app with **React, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS**; integrated with Google Sheets API for live data.  
4. **Validation & Delivery** ‚Äì tested with 15+ students, reduced approval confusion and delays.
